-Types-


secrets could be anything, really.
they could be hidden anywhere...

-Link-


links to a different page.
this may be in-website, or external.

Example:

-Static-


secrets that dont really do much, you do a simple thing, and it`s there. that`s it.
often called an "Easter Egg" despite usually not being Egg-shaped nor Easter-shaped.

-Key-


some secrets function as "keys"- when found, they give users access to new features or other secrets.
(any "key" features are usually minor and/or joke features)

Example:

click to unlock access to 1 air molecule!1!!!

-Real Life-


secrets that you need to go to real life locations to find.
