-Hiding Spots-


Secrets can be just about anywhere.
They could be in text...
or hiding somewhere in an image...
perhaps you need to look through code to find it...
solve puzzles...
or it could be right behind you.

Alright, but seriously, here`s a list of some of the more common ones. this is a BASIC overview, after all!

-Hidden Links-


some text/images may have a link when you click on it... however, It doesn`t indicate that it`s a link, or is very subtle.

Example:

hi

-Encoded-


in some websites, or anything really, there`s codes around... whether it be Morse code, easily translatable with one quick google search (or practice), or multi-layered puzzles of de-contrasted images and spectrograms. (you don`t need to know what those are!)

Example:

(code is 123, in this case)
enter code: ___

(could also be entering the code regardless of whether you are typing anywhere or not)

-Maze-


secrets may be hidden behind sliders, in folders, or just in a generally convoluted to get to spot.

Example:

(drop down folder)
folder - v
folder - v
folder - v
nope
folder - v
nope
folder - v
folder - v
nope
folder - v
good job

-Downloadable-


files containing a secret of some sort. that`s basically it.

Example:

file goes here

-Image Info-


some images contain codes or links...
some obvious,
some you need to do really funky conversions.

Example:

(image goes here: “spoingus”)
